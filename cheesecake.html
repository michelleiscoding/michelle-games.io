Cheesecake

By: Stella D.

Ingredients


1 1/4 cups of gramcraker crumbs
1/4 cups of butter
1/4 cup of sugar
8 ounces of creamcheese
1 cup powdered sugar
1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
1 cup of heavy cream, whiped
12 ounces of cherry pie filling

Instuctions

Mix the gramcraker crums, sugar, and the butter in a bowl then put the crumbly mixture in a pie tin. Then mix togther the sugar, cream cheese, and vanilla. then whisk the heavy cream and mix it with the sugar, cream cheese, and vanilla mixture. Then add the mixture on top of the pie crust and refrigerate for 2-3 hpours then add on the cherry pie filling on top and refrigerate till serving.
